This pungent stench... Yuck... Mysterious tetrafoil pipeworks sprouting from the very earth of petrichor V! Traverse them while fighting for your life! 

![PREVIEW1](https://github.com/WinslowRoR/RoR2-MapsPromoContent/blob/main/StagnantPipeworks/Preview1.png?raw=true)

This mod adds a new Stage 4 with a Post-Loop variant! The "Stagnant Pipeworks" are mysterious ancient pipeworks that seemingly are now a relic of the past no longer in use, only acting as an attraction to come across on your expedition through out the many areas of Petrichor V!

![PREVIEW2](https://github.com/WinslowRoR/RoR2-MapsPromoContent/blob/main/StagnantPipeworks/Preview2.png?raw=true)
